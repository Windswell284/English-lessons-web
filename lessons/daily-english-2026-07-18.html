Trump Threatens New Canada Tariffs Over Wildfire Smoke in US Cities

Today’s Saturday world-affairs lesson looks at cross-border wildfire smoke, trade pressure, climate responsibility, and the diplomatic language used when environmental problems become political disputes.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: Trump threatens new Canada tariffs over wildfire smoke in US cities

Source: BBC News

Published: July 17, 2026

Original article: BBC News article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that US President Donald Trump threatened new tariffs on Canada after wildfire smoke from Canadian fires spread across parts of the northern United States. Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney responded that both countries share responsibility for climate change, while Ontario officials urged US lawmakers to send help rather than complain.

The article says hundreds of fires are burning across Canada, with hazardous smoke affecting cities such as Detroit, Chicago, Washington DC, and New York. Scientists quoted by the BBC say forest management matters near communities, but climate change, hot weather, dry conditions, remote forests, and wind patterns make the problem more complex than blaming one country.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，美國總統川普威脅要對加拿大加徵新關稅，原因是加拿大野火煙霧飄到美國北部多地。加拿大總理卡尼回應說，氣候變遷是兩國共同的責任；安大略省官員則呼籲美國議員與其抱怨，不如提供協助。

文章指出，加拿大目前有數百起野火，危險煙霧影響底特律、芝加哥、華盛頓特區、紐約等城市。BBC 引述科學家表示，森林管理雖然重要，但氣候變遷、炎熱乾燥天氣、偏遠森林地形與風向，都讓問題比單純指責某一國更複雜。

5 Daily Words

smoke

the grey, black, or white gas and tiny particles produced when something burns.

In article: “Smoke from the massive wildfires has cast an orange haze over New York City.”

煙；東西燃燒時產生的灰色、黑色或白色氣體與微粒。

tariffs

taxes that a government charges on goods imported from another country.

In article: “Last year, Trump imposed tariffs on Canada - a country that had enjoyed decades of free trade with the US - and both countries have still yet to reach a trade deal.”

關稅；政府對從其他國家進口的商品所徵收的稅。

hazardous

dangerous, especially because it can harm health, safety, or the environment.

In article: “‘Hazardous’ air quality alerts have been issued across much of the region, leading to the cancellation of many outdoor events.”

危險的；特別指可能危害健康、安全或環境的。

evacuate

to leave a dangerous place, or to make people leave it, for safety.

In article: “Ford said a total of 10 communities have had to evacuate, with many now seeking refuge in southern Ontario cities.”

撤離、疏散；因安全原因離開危險地區，或讓人們離開危險地區。

negligence

failure to take proper care or to do what a responsible person or institution should do.

In article: “Trump said in his post to Truth Social that he would call Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney to demand an explanation over his country's ‘willful negligence.’”

疏忽、怠忽；未盡到應有的注意或責任，尤其是個人或機構本應採取行動卻沒有做到。
