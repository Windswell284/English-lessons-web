Precision Writing: Strong Verbs

Level: intermediateFocus: writing clarityFormat: daily lesson

Core idea

Good English often comes from choosing a precise verb instead of adding many weak words around a vague verb. For example, “make something better” can become improve, refine, strengthen, or optimize, depending on the exact meaning.

Useful verbs

Verb
refine

To improve by making small, careful changes.

Verb
clarify

To make an idea easier to understand.

Verb
strengthen

To make an argument, skill, or structure more powerful.

Verb
condense

To make writing shorter while keeping the main meaning.

Mini practice

Rewrite: “I want to make my writing better.”

Stronger: “I want to refine my writing.” / “I want to strengthen my writing style.”
