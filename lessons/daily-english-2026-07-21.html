US Launches Fresh Strikes on Iran as Trump Warns of Retaliation

Today’s Tuesday world-affairs lesson follows a fast-moving Middle East security story, with vocabulary for military action, escalation, and diplomatic channels.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: US launches fresh strikes on Iran, as Trump warns of retaliation for deaths of soldiers

Source: BBC News

Published: July 20, 2026

Original article: BBC News article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that the US military has begun another round of strikes against Iran, saying the action is intended to reduce Iran’s ability to attack ships in the Strait of Hormuz. The report says the latest attacks followed President Donald Trump’s warning that Iran would be punished for the deaths of US soldiers.

The article also describes a wider regional crisis: Iran says it is in a “full-scale war” with the US, while Jordan and Kuwait report intercepting Iranian missiles or drones. Even as fighting expands, Tehran says diplomatic messages are still moving through intermediaries, showing that military pressure and back-channel communication are happening at the same time.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，美軍已對伊朗展開新一輪攻擊，表示目的是削弱伊朗攻擊荷姆茲海峽船隻的能力。報導指出，最新行動發生在美國總統 Donald Trump 警告伊朗將因美軍士兵死亡而受到懲罰之後。

文章也描述區域危機擴大：伊朗稱自己正與美國進入「全面戰爭」，約旦與科威特則表示攔截了伊朗飛彈或無人機。即使戰事升高，德黑蘭仍說雙方透過中間人交換外交訊息，顯示軍事壓力與幕後溝通同時存在。

5 Daily Words

strikes

military attacks, especially attacks carried out from the air or with missiles.

In article: “The US military says it has begun a new round of strikes against Iran to ‘further degrade’ its capabilities to attack ships in the Strait of Hormuz.”

軍事打擊、攻擊行動；特別指空襲、飛彈攻擊或其他有計畫的軍事行動。

retaliation

action taken to punish or respond to an attack, insult, or harmful act.

In article: “US launches fresh strikes on Iran, as Trump warns of retaliation for deaths of soldiers.”

報復、反擊；因受到攻擊、傷害或挑釁而採取的懲罰性回應。

capabilities

the practical abilities, resources, or systems that allow a country, army, or organization to do something.

In article: “The US says the attacks were aimed at degrading Iran’s capabilities to attack ships in the Strait of Hormuz.”

能力、作戰能力、資源條件；使國家、軍隊或組織能執行某項行動的實際能力與系統。

intermediaries

people, governments, or organizations that carry messages or help communication between sides that are not dealing directly with each other.

In article: “Tehran said diplomatic messages were still being exchanged by both sides through intermediaries, despite a collapse of a ceasefire.”

中間人、居間者；在雙方無法或不願直接接觸時，協助傳遞訊息或溝通的人、政府或組織。

hazardous

dangerous or involving serious risk, especially in military, environmental, or transport contexts.

In article: “Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps said the tankers were going through what it called an ‘unsafe and hazardous southern route’ of the strait.”

危險的、有風險的；常用於軍事、環境、交通或安全情境，表示可能造成嚴重傷害或損失。
