AI Summaries of Tripadvisor Hotel Reviews Downplay Serious Complaints

Today’s Thursday technology-and-AI lesson looks at automated review summaries, consumer trust, and the formal language journalists use when a platform’s AI tool softens serious warnings.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: AI summaries of Tripadvisor hotel reviews downplay serious complaints, investigation finds

Source: The Guardian

Published: July 2, 2026

Original article: The Guardian article link

Article Summary

The Guardian reports on a Which? investigation into AI-generated hotel review summaries on Tripadvisor. The investigation found that some automated overviews softened or missed serious guest complaints, including allegations of sexual harassment, poor hygiene, illness, mould, and unreliable water.

Tripadvisor said it was monitoring and refining the AI tool, and argued that the summaries are meant to help travellers understand broad feedback while still reading the underlying reviews. Consumer experts warned that users should check detailed guest reviews, especially low-rated ones, before booking.

文章摘要

《衛報》報導，消費者組織 Which? 調查 Tripadvisor 的 AI 旅館評論摘要後發現，一些自動生成的概覽淡化或漏掉了嚴重投訴，包括性騷擾指控、衛生問題、旅客生病、發霉氣味，以及供水不穩等情況。

Tripadvisor 表示，公司正在監測並改進這項 AI 工具，並稱摘要的目的，是幫助旅客快速理解大量回饋，同時仍可閱讀底下的完整評論。消費者專家則提醒，訂房前應查看詳細旅客評論，尤其是一星評價。

5 Daily Words

reviews

written opinions or evaluations of a product, service, hotel, film, or experience.

In article: “AI summaries of Tripadvisor hotel reviews downplay serious complaints, investigation finds”

評論、評價；對產品、服務、旅館、電影或某種經驗所寫下的意見或評估。

summaries

short versions of longer information that keep only the main points.

In article: “Tripadvisor said its systems automatically suppress AI summaries when travellers warn about serious safety incidents...”

摘要、概要；把較長資訊濃縮成只保留重點的簡短版本。

allegations

claims that someone has done something wrong, usually before the claims have been proved in court or by investigation.

In article: “AI-generated overview found to gloss over allegations of sexual harassment and describes hotel being sued over hygiene as ‘spotless’”

指控、說法；通常指某人做錯事的主張，但尚未由法院或調查正式證實。

refining

improving something by making small, careful changes so it becomes more accurate, useful, or effective.

In article: “Tripadvisor said it was monitoring and refining its AI tool...”

改進、精修；透過小而仔細的調整，讓某項工具、流程或想法更準確、更有用或更有效。

sanitise

to make something seem cleaner, safer, or less negative by removing unpleasant, controversial, or forceful details.

In article: “He found AI tends to ‘sanitise and rub off the edges’ of sharper criticisms...”

淡化、淨化、修飾；刪除令人不快、有爭議或尖銳的細節，使某事看起來更乾淨、安全或不那麼負面。

Usage note: This is today’s stretch word. In technology and media reporting, it often means making criticism sound too mild.
