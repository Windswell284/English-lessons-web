Fresh Doubts Over Microsoft’s Quantum Computing Claims

Today’s technology lesson uses a BBC News report about Microsoft’s quantum-computing research, scientific criticism, and how experts test big claims in a fast-moving field.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: Scientist publishes fresh doubts over Microsoft’s quantum claims

Source: BBC News

Published: June 24, 2026

Original article: BBC article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that UK-based physicist Dr Henry Legg has published new criticism of Microsoft’s quantum-computing research in the journal Nature. He argues that a software tool used to check the company’s work had coding errors and was not accurate enough to support some of Microsoft’s conclusions.

Microsoft says it stands by its research and has defended its approach, while acknowledging that scepticism and rigour are part of science. The dispute matters because quantum computing is a high-stakes industry: companies hope the technology could one day solve problems too complex for today’s most powerful supercomputers.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，英國物理學家 Henry Legg 博士在《自然》期刊發表新論文，質疑微軟的量子運算研究。他認為，微軟用來檢查研究結果的一項軟體工具存在程式錯誤，而且準確度不足以支撐部分結論。

微軟表示仍堅持自己的研究成果，並強調懷疑與嚴謹是科學過程的一部分。這場爭議之所以重要，是因為量子運算已成為高風險、高投資的科技領域；企業希望未來能用它解決現有超級電腦也難以處理的複雜問題。

5 Daily Words

claims

statements that something is true, especially when other people may question or test them.

In article: “Scientist publishes fresh doubts over Microsoft’s quantum claims.”

聲稱、主張；某人或某機構表示為真的說法，尤其是仍可能被質疑或驗證的說法。

Usage note: In science and business news, claims often need evidence before the public accepts them.

scepticism

an attitude of doubting whether something is true or reliable until there is strong evidence.

In article: “The tech giant has consistently stood by its conclusions despite scepticism from experts in the field.”

懷疑態度；在有充分證據之前，對某件事是否真實或可靠保持保留。

assertion

a confident statement that something is true, even if it still needs proof.

In article: “Legg also said Microsoft had still not proved its assertion that it has managed to create a theoretical quasi-particle called Majorana.”

斷言、明確主張；很有把握地說某事為真，但仍可能需要證明。

Usage note: Assertion is more formal than claim and is common in legal, academic, and policy writing.

scrutinise

to examine something very carefully, especially to find errors, weaknesses, or hidden problems.

In article: “Legg also accused Microsoft of not sharing enough data for other scientists to scrutinise.”

仔細審查、嚴格檢視；特別是為了找出錯誤、弱點或隱藏問題。

arbitration

the process of asking an independent person or organisation to judge a dispute and help decide the outcome.

In article: “Microsoft said it was sharing all of its data with the US defence agency Darpa for independent arbitration.”

仲裁；請獨立第三方判斷爭議並協助決定結果的程序。

Usage note: This is today’s stretch word. It is common in legal, business, and international-dispute contexts.
