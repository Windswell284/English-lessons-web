UAE Condemns Iran’s ‘Brazen’ Tanker Attack as US Launches Fresh Strikes

Today’s Tuesday world-affairs lesson follows a fast-moving security story about the Strait of Hormuz, maritime trade, and the language of escalation.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: UAE condemns Iran's 'brazen' attack on tankers as US launches fresh strikes

Source: BBC News

Published: July 13, 2026

Original article: BBC News article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that the United Arab Emirates accused Iran of a “brazen” attack on two tankers in the Strait of Hormuz, killing one crew member and wounding several others. The report says the incident came as the United States launched another night of strikes on Iran and announced a new charge connected to a blockade of the strategic waterway.

The article shows how quickly a maritime security incident can become a wider diplomatic and economic crisis. Both Washington and Tehran are using strong language about control of the Strait, while the waterway remains vital for global oil shipping and a major flashpoint between the US, Iran, and regional allies.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，阿拉伯聯合大公國指控伊朗在荷莫茲海峽對兩艘油輪發動「公然」攻擊，造成一名船員死亡、多人受傷。報導指出，事件發生之際，美國又對伊朗展開新一輪空襲，並宣布與封鎖這條戰略水道相關的新收費措施。

這篇文章顯示，海上安全事件可能迅速升高為更大的外交與經濟危機。華盛頓與德黑蘭都以強硬措辭談論對海峽的控制，而這條水道對全球石油運輸至關重要，也是美國、伊朗與區域盟友之間的重要衝突焦點。

5 Daily Words

strikes

military attacks, especially quick attacks from the air or with missiles.

In article: “The US president said they were hitting Iran ‘very hard’ as strikes were launched for the third consecutive night amid escalating attacks between the two countries.”

軍事打擊、攻擊行動；特別指空襲或飛彈等快速軍事攻擊。

blockade

an action that uses military or political power to stop ships, goods, or people from entering or leaving an area.

In article: “The UAE accused Iran of a ‘brazen’ attack on two tankers in the Strait of Hormuz on Monday, killing one and wounding eight, as Donald Trump said the US would impose a 20% charge as part of a new blockade on the waterway.”

封鎖；以軍事或政治力量阻止船隻、貨物或人員進出某個地區的行動。

flashpoint

a place, issue, or moment where conflict could suddenly become more serious.

In article: “The strait remains a key flashpoint between the US and Iran, with both sides clashing over the waterway's control on Monday after exchanging strikes in the region over Sunday night.”

衝突熱點、導火線；可能使衝突突然升高的地點、議題或時刻。

constitutes

formally amounts to something; is considered to be a particular kind of act, condition, or problem.

In article: “The Ministry of Defense condemns this brazen attack, which constitutes a serious violation and a clear breach of international law, threatening the security and stability of the region,” it said.

構成、等同於；正式表示某行為或狀況可被視為某種問題、違規或條件。

reimbursed

paid back for money already spent, especially when an organization claims compensation for costs.

In article: “The U.S.A. will be... reimbursed, at the rate of 20% on all cargo shipped, for any and all costs necessary to do the job of providing safety and security...”

獲得補償、報銷；特別指組織因已支出的費用而被支付補償款。
