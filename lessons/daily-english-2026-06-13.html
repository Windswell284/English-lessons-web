Current Events English: Hormuz Reopening Deal

Today’s lesson uses a BBC News current-events report about diplomacy, fighting, and the Strait of Hormuz to study concise international-news vocabulary.

Level: intermediateFocus: diplomacy vocabularyTime: 5–10 minutes
Article base

Title: “Deal to end fighting would lead to Hormuz reopening, Iran says”

Source: BBC News

Published: June 12, 2026

Read the original article: BBC News article link

Article base: title/source link + short learning snippets only.

Article Summary

BBC News reported that Iran said a deal to end fighting would lead to the reopening of Hormuz. The BBC RSS summary also said the deal was close to being finalised, according to the US, Iran, and mediators Pakistan.

For English learners, this story is useful because diplomatic news often uses compact phrases such as pave the way, hostilities, and mediators to describe negotiations and conflict.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，伊朗表示，如果達成結束戰鬥的協議，霍爾木茲海峽將會重新開放。BBC 的 RSS 摘要也提到，美國、伊朗與調停方巴基斯坦表示，該協議已接近完成。

這篇新聞適合學習外交與衝突相關英文，因為國際新聞常用精簡表達，例如 pave the way（為……鋪路／使……成為可能）、hostilities（敵對行動、戰鬥）與 mediators（調停者）。

Key Words

Noun
deal

Original headline: “Deal to end fighting would lead to Hormuz reopening, Iran says”

English definition: an agreement between people, groups, or countries.

中文定義：協議、交易、安排；在國際新聞中通常指各方達成的政治或外交協議。

Usage note: In headlines, deal often means a negotiated agreement, not a shopping discount.

Verb phrase
pave the way

Original RSS excerpt: “The deal which will pave the way for hostilities to end is close to being finalised, the US, Iran and mediators Pakistan say.”

English definition: to make it possible or easier for something else to happen.

中文定義：為某事鋪路、使某事成為可能；常用於描述某個決定或協議帶來下一步結果。

Noun
hostilities

Original RSS excerpt: “The deal which will pave the way for hostilities to end is close to being finalised, the US, Iran and mediators Pakistan say.”

English definition: fighting or acts of war between opposing sides.

中文定義：敵對行動、戰鬥、交戰狀態；多用複數，常見於正式新聞與外交語境。

Usage note: Hostilities is more formal than fighting.

Adjective phrase
close to being finalised

Original RSS excerpt: “The deal which will pave the way for hostilities to end is close to being finalised, the US, Iran and mediators Pakistan say.”

English definition: nearly finished or almost officially completed.

中文定義：接近敲定、即將正式完成；finalised 是英式拼法，美式常寫作 finalized。

Noun
mediators

Original RSS excerpt: “The deal which will pave the way for hostilities to end is close to being finalised, the US, Iran and mediators Pakistan say.”

English definition: people, countries, or organizations that help opposing sides reach an agreement.

中文定義：調停者、斡旋方；協助衝突雙方溝通並尋求協議的人、國家或組織。

Noun phrase
reopening

Original headline: “Deal to end fighting would lead to Hormuz reopening, Iran says”

English definition: the act of opening again after being closed or restricted.

中文定義：重新開放；表示某個地點、通道、服務或活動在關閉後再次開放。

Word Origins & Notes

Mediator: This word comes from the idea of being “in the middle.” A mediator stands between opposing sides to help them communicate.

Hostilities: The related word hostile means unfriendly or opposed. In news English, hostilities usually means actual conflict or fighting.

Pave the way: The image is road-building: if you pave a road, travel becomes easier. In politics, it means one step makes the next step possible.
