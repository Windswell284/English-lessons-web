How Aldi Is Taking On US Supermarkets With Its $4 Almond Butter

Today’s Monday business lesson looks at discount retail, urban expansion, and the vocabulary journalists use when a challenger brand tests a huge market.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: How Aldi is taking on US supermarkets with its $4 almond butter

Source: BBC News

Published: July 12, 2026

Original article: BBC News article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that German discount supermarket Aldi is expanding aggressively in the United States, including a new Manhattan store where shoppers are finding much cheaper everyday products. The company plans to add 800 US stores over five years as part of a $9bn push into dense urban markets.

The article explains that Aldi has already changed grocery competition in the UK and Europe, but the US market is harder because Walmart is much larger and has enormous advantages in technology, advertising, membership revenue, and supply chains. Analysts say Aldi can still win customers by keeping a lean model, controlling costs, and offering lower prices during a period of persistent inflation.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，德國折扣超市 Aldi 正積極擴張美國市場，包括在曼哈頓開設新店，讓顧客買到價格低很多的日常食品。Aldi 計畫在五年內於美國新增 800 間店，這是 90 億美元拓展計畫的一部分，重點放在人口密集的都市市場。

文章指出，Aldi 已經改變英國與歐洲的食品零售競爭，但美國市場更困難，因為 Walmart 規模巨大，並在科技、廣告、會員收入與供應鏈方面擁有龐大優勢。分析師認為，在通膨持續的環境下，Aldi 仍可透過精簡模式、控制成本與低價策略吸引顧客。

5 Daily Words

bargains

things bought for less than their usual or expected price; good-value purchases.

In article: “When Mary Porter walked into Manhattan's newest Aldi store hunting for bargains, the long-time resident found what she considered a retail miracle in plain sight...”

便宜貨、划算的商品；以低於一般或預期價格買到、很有價值的東西。

expansion

the process of becoming larger, wider, or more active in a market or area.

In article: “Porter's discovery is part of Aldi's $9bn US expansion plan to add 800 new stores over five years...”

擴張、拓展；在市場或地區變得更大、更廣或更活躍的過程。

incumbent

already holding a position, role, or market advantage when a challenger arrives.

In article: “Incumbent US grocers may look with some concern at the insurgency Aldi pulled off since it entered the UK market in the 1990s.”

現任的、既有的；在挑戰者出現前已占有職位、角色或市場優勢的。

overheads

the regular costs of running a business, such as rent, staff, utilities, and operations.

In article: “This strict reliance on limited, private labels is exactly what keeps Aldi's overheads low, according to Dustin York...”

營運開銷、間接成本；經營企業所需的固定或日常成本，例如租金、人事、水電與營運支出。

insurgency

a forceful challenge against an established power; in business writing, a disruptive push by a challenger company.

In article: “Incumbent US grocers may look with some concern at the insurgency Aldi pulled off since it entered the UK market in the 1990s.”

反叛、挑戰既有勢力；在商業報導中，可指挑戰者企業對既有市場力量發起的強勢衝擊。
