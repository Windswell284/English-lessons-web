US Strikes Iran After Cargo Ship Attack

Today’s world affairs lesson uses a BBC News report about US military strikes on Iranian targets after a cargo ship was hit near the Strait of Hormuz.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: US conducts strikes on Iran after attack on cargo ship

Source: BBC News

Published: June 27, 2026

Original article: BBC article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that the US military struck Iranian missile, drone, and coastal radar sites after a cargo ship was hit by a one-way attack drone near the Strait of Hormuz. No casualties were reported, but the incident raised concerns about shipping safety in a vital international trade route.

US Central Command called the action a response to aggression against commercial shipping, while Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps accused the US of breaking its commitments. The report places the episode within recent tensions over ceasefire arrangements, maritime law, and the movement of commercial vessels through the strait.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，一艘貨船在荷姆茲海峽附近遭單向攻擊無人機擊中後，美軍攻擊了伊朗的飛彈、無人機與沿岸雷達設施。事件中無人傷亡，但它再次引發外界對這條重要國際貿易航道航運安全的擔憂。

美國中央司令部稱這次行動是對商業航運遭受攻擊的回應；伊朗伊斯蘭革命衛隊則指控美國違反承諾。報導也把此事放在近期停火安排、海事法，以及商船通過海峽等緊張議題的背景下說明。

5 Daily Words

strikes

military attacks, especially ones carried out quickly against specific targets.

In article: “The US military has conducted strikes on Iranian targets after President Donald Trump accused Iran of a ‘foolish violation’ of its truce following an attack on a cargo ship in the Strait of Hormuz.”

軍事打擊、攻擊；通常指針對特定目標迅速發動的軍事行動。

violation

an action that breaks a rule, law, agreement, or promise.

In article: “The US military has conducted strikes on Iranian targets after President Donald Trump accused Iran of a ‘foolish violation’ of its truce following an attack on a cargo ship in the Strait of Hormuz.”

違反、違規；指破壞規則、法律、協議或承諾的行為。

evacuation

the organized movement of people away from a dangerous place to somewhere safer.

In article: “No casualties were reported when the ship was struck by a one-way attack drone on Thursday, an incident that prompted a planned evacuation of thousands of sailors stuck in the region.”

疏散、撤離；有組織地把人從危險地區移到較安全的地方。

aggression

hostile or forceful behavior, especially by a state, military, or group.

In article: “The unwarranted aggression against commercial shipping by Iranian forces clearly violated the ceasefire,” it said in a statement.

侵略、攻擊性行為；尤指國家、軍隊或團體採取的敵對或強硬行動。

memorandum

a formal written note or document, often recording an agreement, policy, or official understanding.

In article: “The US and Iran agreed on 17 June to end hostilities under a 14-point memorandum of understanding, which had also called for Iran to use its ‘best efforts for the safe passage of commercial vessels with no charge for 60 days’.”

備忘錄；正式書面文件，常用來記錄協議、政策或官方共識。

Usage note: This is today’s stretch word. In diplomacy and business, memorandum of understanding is often shortened to MOU.
