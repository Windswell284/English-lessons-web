Brunello Cucinelli and the Art of Slower Work

Today’s lighter culture-and-business lesson uses Om Malik’s long-form conversation with Brunello Cucinelli about cashmere, craftsmanship, humane capitalism, and protecting time away from work.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: Brunello Cucinelli

Source: On my Om

Published: April 27, 2015

Original article: On my Om article link

Article Summary

Om Malik visits Solomeo, Italy, to meet Brunello Cucinelli, the fashion entrepreneur often called the “king of cashmere.” The interview is less about fashion trends and more about Cucinelli’s unusual way of building a luxury company around craftsmanship, fair wages, beauty, and what he calls human dignity.

Cucinelli describes a slower philosophy of work: no emails after 5:30 PM, no phones in meetings, fewer interruptions, and more time for reflection. The piece feels light and reflective, but it also gives useful vocabulary for talking about design, business ethics, attention, and a more balanced life.

文章摘要

Om Malik 到義大利 Solomeo 拜訪 Brunello Cucinelli。Cucinelli 常被稱為「喀什米爾之王」，但這篇訪談不只是談時尚，而是談他如何把工藝、公平薪資、美感，以及「人的尊嚴」放在企業核心。

Cucinelli 提出一種較慢、較有餘裕的工作哲學：下午五點半後不寄電子郵件、開會不帶手機、減少打斷，並保留思考與休息的時間。文章輕鬆而有啟發性，也適合學習設計、商業倫理、專注力與生活平衡相關的英文詞彙。

5 Daily Words

cashmere

a very soft, fine wool used in expensive sweaters, scarves, and coats.

In article: “Dubbed the ‘king of cashmere’ by The New Yorker, Brunello Cucinelli is the founder of the eponymously named fashion house.”

喀什米爾羊毛；一種非常柔軟、細緻，常用於高級毛衣、圍巾與外套的羊毛材質。

dignity

the state of being respected and treated as valuable, serious, and human.

In article: “He gives 20 percent of his company’s profits to his charitable foundation in the name of ‘human dignity’.”

尊嚴；指一個人被尊重、被認真看待，並被視為有價值的狀態。

artisans

skilled workers who make things carefully by hand, often using traditional methods.

In article: “Do you want a company that grows in a gracious way? That allows suppliers to grow alongside it, so that your artisans can grow as well as the company’s staff?”

工匠、手藝人；以手工和專業技術製作物品的人，常帶有傳統工藝的意味。

transparency

openness and honesty that make actions, decisions, or information easy to see and judge.

In article: “So you are in favor of radical transparency?”

透明度、公開透明；讓行動、決策或資訊容易被看見與檢驗的開放狀態。

preposterous

completely unreasonable, ridiculous, or shockingly excessive.

In article: “I do not buy a specific product if I know you have made preposterous amounts of profit out of it.”

荒謬的、離譜的；指非常不合理、可笑，或過度到令人驚訝的。

Usage note: This is today’s stretch word. It is stronger and more formal than silly or ridiculous.
