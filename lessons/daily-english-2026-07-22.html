More Women in Their 20s Are Getting Type 2 Diabetes

Today’s Wednesday health lesson looks at a BBC report on rising type 2 diabetes among younger adults in England, with useful vocabulary for public-health trends, diagnosis, and risk.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Source article

Title: More women in their 20s are getting type 2 diabetes

Source: BBC News

Published: July 22, 2026

Original article: BBC News article link

Article Summary

BBC News reports that England is seeing a worrying rise in type 2 diabetes among women in their 20s. The article says the increase has largely happened since the Covid pandemic and is closely linked to rising obesity levels, based on analysis of NHS data from 2011 to 2024.

The report explains that type 2 diabetes can cause serious complications such as heart attacks and strokes, especially when it develops at a younger age. Experts say early diagnosis, weight management, healthy eating advice, and newer obesity treatments could help reduce long-term organ damage and improve outcomes.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，英格蘭二十多歲女性罹患第二型糖尿病的人數正在令人擔憂地上升。文章指出，這個增加大多發生在新冠疫情之後，並且根據 2011 至 2024 年的 NHS 資料分析，與肥胖程度上升密切相關。

報導說明，第二型糖尿病可能造成心臟病發作、中風等嚴重併發症，若在較年輕時發病，風險尤其值得注意。專家表示，及早診斷、體重管理、健康飲食建議，以及新型肥胖治療，可能有助於減少長期器官損傷並改善健康結果。

5 Daily Words

symptoms

signs in the body or mind that may show someone has an illness or medical condition.

In article: “The most common symptoms include feeling very tired, peeing more than usual and feeling thirsty all the time.”

症狀；身體或心理出現、可能表示某人有疾病或健康問題的徵象。

complications

additional medical problems that make an illness more serious or harder to treat.

In article: “Type 2 diabetes is serious, and can lead to severe complications, such as heart attacks and strokes.”

併發症；使原本疾病變得更嚴重或更難治療的額外健康問題。

remission

a period when the signs of a disease become weaker or disappear, even if doctors still monitor the condition.

In article: “Type 2 diabetes can sometimes be put into remission by eating a healthy diet and keeping slim without needing to rely on glucose-lowering medications.”

緩解期、病情緩解；疾病徵象減輕或消失，但仍需要追蹤管理的狀態。

diagnoses

formal identifications of diseases or conditions, usually made by medical professionals after tests or examination.

In article: “Risk increases with age and older adults still make up the largest proportion of new diagnoses.”

診斷結果、確診案例；通常由醫療專業人員經檢查或檢驗後正式判定的疾病或健康狀況。

incidence

the rate or frequency at which something, especially a disease, happens in a particular group or place.

In article: “We now see that incidence is also increasing in white populations too, showing that this public health challenge is broadening across the generations.”

發生率；某種事件或疾病在特定族群、地區中出現的頻率或比例。
