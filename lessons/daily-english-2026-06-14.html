US-Iran Deal Timing

Today’s lesson uses a BBC News current-events item about a possible US-Iran agreement and careful wording around diplomacy, uncertainty, and scheduling.

Level: intermediateTime: 5–10 minutes
Article base

Title: “Trump says US-Iran deal to be signed on Sunday as Tehran casts doubt on timing”

Source: BBC News

Published: June 13, 2026

Original article: BBC News article link

Article Summary

BBC News reported that President Trump said a US-Iran deal would be signed on Sunday, while Tehran questioned that timing. The BBC RSS summary said Iran had not confirmed an exact date and said it “will not be tomorrow.”

For English learners, the item is useful because diplomatic reporting often uses careful verbs and phrases—such as casts doubt, timing, and exact date—to show uncertainty without using emotional language.

文章摘要

BBC News 報導，川普表示美國與伊朗的協議將於週日簽署，但德黑蘭方面對這個時間點提出疑問。BBC 的 RSS 摘要指出，伊朗表示尚未決定確切日期，並稱「不會是明天」。

這則新聞適合學習外交新聞英文，因為報導常用精準而克制的詞語，例如 casts doubt（使人懷疑）、timing（時間安排）與 exact date（確切日期），來表達不確定性。

5 Daily Words

deal

an agreement between people, groups, companies, or countries.

In article: “Trump says US-Iran deal to be signed on Sunday as Tehran casts doubt on timing”

協議、交易、安排；在外交新聞中通常指國家之間談成的協議。

Usage note: In politics, deal usually means a negotiated agreement, not a shopping discount.

casts doubt

makes something seem uncertain, questionable, or less likely to be true.

In article: “Trump says US-Iran deal to be signed on Sunday as Tehran casts doubt on timing”

使人懷疑、讓某事變得不確定；常用於新聞標題，表示某方對說法或計畫提出疑問。

timing

the choice or arrangement of when something happens.

In article: “Trump says US-Iran deal to be signed on Sunday as Tehran casts doubt on timing”

時機、時間安排；可指某件事何時發生，或安排得是否合適。

Usage note: Timing is broader than time; it often includes planning and strategy.

exact date

the specific calendar day when something will happen.

In article: “The US president's comments come as Iran says an exact date has not been decided, but it ‘will not be tomorrow’.”

確切日期、明確的日子；用來強調不是大概時間，而是具體哪一天。

decided

chosen or settled after consideration.

In article: “The US president's comments come as Iran says an exact date has not been decided, but it ‘will not be tomorrow’.”

已決定、已敲定；表示經過考慮後做出選擇或安排。
